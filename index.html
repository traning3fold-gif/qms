Course Overview

The ISO 9001:2015 Lead Auditor Course is designed to provide professionals with the knowledge and skills required to perform first, second, and third-party audits of Quality Management Systems.

Delivered by industry experts, this comprehensive 40-hour course equips you with auditing techniques, practical methodologies, and the confidence to lead an audit team successfully.

What You Will Learn

  • Purpose and benefits of a Quality Management System.
  • Role of an auditor to plan, conduct, report and follow up a QMS audit.
  • Plan-Do-Check-Act (PDCA) cycle application in QMS.
  • ISO 9001:2015 requirements deep dive.
  • Techniques for gathering objective evidence.
  • Leading an audit team and managing audit communications.

Course Curriculum

Day 1: Introduction to QMS

Fundamentals of Quality, PDCA Cycle, and Overview of ISO 9001:2015 framework.

Day 2: Auditing Principles

Types of audits, auditor responsibilities, and deep dive into standard clauses.

Day 3: Audit Preparation

Document review, creating audit plans, and developing effective checklists.

Day 4: Conducting the Audit

Opening meetings, interviewing techniques, identifying non-conformities.

Day 5: Reporting & Assessment

Closing meetings, writing audit reports, follow-up actions, and the final CQI IRCA exam.
